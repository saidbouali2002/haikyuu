Shôyô is a small volleyball player. Despite this handicap, he manages to compete against his opponents thanks to his incredible jumping. During his first tournament in middle school, he is humiliated by Tobio, a pretentious but talented player. Once in high school, he decides to take his revenge. When he joins the team, he discovers that his rival will become his teammate. This is how one of the most prodigious duos in volleyball was born.
